This study will ask you to:

  1. Sign up for an Account
  2. Answer questions about your health
  3. Complete simple activities like walking or tapping on the screen
  4. Provide data

This study is designed for persons over 18 years old with or without Parkinson Disease.

 

1. Sign up for an Account
To sign up for an account you must first complete a consent process explaining the risks and benefits of the study and also confirm your agreement to participate in the study. Afterward, we will ask you to create an account, and verify your email address and other general information about you.

2. Complete Health Surveys
We will ask you to answer questions about yourself, your medical history, and current health. In addition we will ask you daily, weekly and monthly questions about your symptoms. You can leave any questions that you do not wish to answer blank.

3. Complete Simple Activities
We will ask you to perform specific tasks while holding or using your mobile phone. For example:

  • Saying “aaah” for 10 seconds into your iPhone to record variations in your voice
  • Walking 20 steps forward or standing still with your iPhone in your pocket to assess your gait
  • Tapping on the phone screen repeatedly to test your motor speed and coordination

These activities should take about 20 minutes a day.

We will send notices on your phone asking you to complete these tasks and surveys. You may choose to act at your convenience, participate in all or only in some parts of the study and skip any questions you do not wish to answer.
